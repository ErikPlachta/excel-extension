## Sideload Office Add-ins to Office on the web

To test your Office Add-in during development, you can sideload it into an Office application like Excel. Sideloading allows you to load your add-in directly from a manifest file without publishing it to the Office Store.

https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/office/dev/add-ins/testing/sideload-office-add-ins-for-testing
